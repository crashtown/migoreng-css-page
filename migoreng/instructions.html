Instructions

Please follow the steps below to make your MiGoreng Noodles.

1. Boil Noodles

Unpack noodles and put in container, add water, microwave for 4 minutes and drain once done.

2. Add flavours/Ingredients

Add flavour sachets, and add extra ingredients! Mix Well.

3. Enjoy!
